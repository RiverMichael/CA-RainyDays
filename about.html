The story of Rainydays

Our journey started back in 2004 when our founders Michael and Joey had challenges with there equipment during a hike in the foothills of mount Tibidabo in Spain.

After there trip they started talking about what improvments you could make to the equipment they used, especially the jackets.

Since Joey was working in product development and Michael worked in marketing that conversation was the beginning to the journey of RainyDays.
